Comprehensive Logistics Services

DS Logistics provides a robust network and end-to-end capabilities across every major mode of transit.

Ocean Freight (FCL/LCL)

Reliable ocean freight solutions with fixed departures. We manage negotiations, container consolidation, and terminal tracking.

  • Direct contract rates with major ocean alliances
  • Strategic consolidation centers globally
  • Drayage and last-mile port clearance

Air Freight

Express air cargo scheduling for urgent supply lines. Access key routing space with direct customs synchronization.

  • Next-flight-out expedited routing
  • Temperature-controlled cool chain logs
  • Door-to-door tracking & delivery clearances

Warehousing & Distribution

Scalable storage space at core global trade nodes. Automated pick-pack routing, fulfillment, and container cross-docking.

  • Bonded storage & customs warehouses
  • API-integrated warehouse inventory
  • E-commerce replenishment routing

Built for Modern Global Supply Chains

DS Logistics provides a robust technology suite that empowers shippers to navigate complex trade lanes with confidence.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Monitor ocean container coordinates, port milestones, vessel telemetry, and predictive arrival times from origin to destination.

Automated Customs

DS Logistics' digital broker engine parses customs documents, maps HS codes, and submits filings directly to port agencies in minutes.

Actionable Trade Data

Optimize supply chains with structured reports on landing costs, port congestion, carrier transit averages, and warehouse stock levels.

Sustainable Carbon Offsets
